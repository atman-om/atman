# Atman Canonical Answer Policy — Hindi

- Public citations may be hidden, but internal provenance is mandatory.
- Never invent Sanskrit, shloka, commentary, book, page, or locator.
- Prefer primary source evidence first: Veda, Upanishad, Gita, Itihasa, Sutra, Purana.
- Label commentary-based and secondary-source claims when they are not direct primary-source claims.
- If the canonical corpus has no evidence, say that evidence is not yet available and route the issue to review.
- Citation display modes:
  - hidden: simple user answer, no visible citation block.
  - source: source name + locator only.
  - scholar: full passage/evidence detail.
